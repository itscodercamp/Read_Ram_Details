About The Organization

Our History

The history of Rahbar-e-Faiz-e-Aam is a testament to the enduring spirit of empowerment and service. Founded by Bilal Qasmi, our journey began with a vision to transform lives, uplift communities, and nurture minds across India. Over the years, we have steadfastly upheld our commitment to education, linguistic diversity, and religious upliftment. Our legacy is marked by the establishment of Madarsas, where generations of young minds have been equipped with the tools of knowledge and languages, transcending cultural boundaries. Together, we aim to illuminate the path to a brighter, more inclusive future.

We take pride in our role as custodians of religious institutions, constructing Masjids and fostering spiritual growth. Our support for daily prayers and special religious events has deepened the bonds of faith and community. Education, from primary to higher levels, has been a cornerstone of our mission. Scholarships and self-employment initiatives have empowered countless individuals. As we reflect on our history, we carry forward the legacy of Bilal Qasmi, inspired by the sacrifices of national leaders, and driven by a commitment to empower rural communities. Our history is a story of resilience, compassion, and unwavering dedication to a brighter, more enlightened India.
