Who Are We?

We are Rahbar-e-Faiz-e-Aam, founded by Bilal Qasmi, a dynamic force dedicated to education, spirituality, and societal betterment in India. Our journey is defined by a commitment to linguistic diversity, religious upliftment, comprehensive education, and empowerment. Together, we aim to illuminate the path to a brighter, more inclusive future.

Founded by - Mufti Bilal Qasmi
